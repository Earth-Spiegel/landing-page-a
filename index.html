About Us

Frank is a Navy and Merchant Marine veteran with a bachelor of science in Marine Engineering Technology. He worked on ships fixing all sorts of equipment for over 16 years before retiring from the sea-going life. He is a graduate of NRVTA and a CERTIFIED RV TECHNICIAN with over two years of experience troubleshooting, repairing and maintaining Recreational Vehicles.
He often works with his wife Onthana, and the two of them are a good team. He's fair, honest, dependable, easy to work with, and has a good work ethic. He chose this profession for the second half of life because he loves using his skills to help people.
We look forward to meeting you and servicing your RV needs.
